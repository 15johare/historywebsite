Historians and Archeologists

What is archeaology

Archeaology is the study of what has been left behind by people from the past. Archeaologists dig up the ground to find clues left by our ancestors. These can vary between bones and objects people have made. The man-made objects they find are called artefacts. These include jewllery, pottery, tools, weapons and buildings.
